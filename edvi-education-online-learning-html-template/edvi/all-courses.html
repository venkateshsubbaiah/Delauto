    Our Courses

Autocad

This course offers an in-depth exploration of AutoCAD Mechanical, a specialized toolset of AutoCAD tailored for mechanical engineering.

It covers essential features for drafting, designing, and modeling mechanical components and assemblies. Students will learn to utilize the software to enhance productivity and streamline mechanical design processes.

Solidworks

This course provides an extensive exploration of SolidWorks, a leading CAD software for mechanical design and simulation.

Participants will learn how to create complex 3D models, assemblies, and detailed drawings. The course also covers the simulation tools within SolidWorks, enabling students to analyze and optimize their designs for performance and manufacturability.

Know More

Catia

This course delves into CATIA (Computer Aided Three-Dimensional Interactive Application), a leading software suite for CAD, CAM, and CAE developed by Dassault Systèmes.

The program focuses on equipping students with the skills to design, simulate, and analyze complex mechanical systems and products. From conceptual sketches to detailed parts and assemblies, this course covers the full spectrum of CATIA’s capabilities.

Know More

Nx CAD

Siemens NX CAD is a popular course that is used for mechanical production design and manufacturing.

This course provides a detailed exploration of Siemens NX CAD, a leading integrated solution for CAD. NX CAD is known for its powerful capabilities in product design and engineering. The program covers the comprehensive functionalists of NX CAD, from basic 3D modelling to advanced modelling. Candidates will gain the skills necessary to use NX CAD for designing, optimizing, and validating complex mechanical systems.
